THE MISSION

Our goal is simple: engage the disengaged by fusing internet culture with political advertising. We're focused on amplifying the voices of those working towards a better future, and inspiring a new era of informed citizens. With our wit, creativity, and expertise in media production, we're confident in our ability to bring about real change in the world.

Beyond that, we're more than just a media production company. Our roots run deep in a passion for political change and the desire to amplify the voices of marginalized communities. Our co-founders, Justin and Chris, bring a wealth of experience and expertise to the table. Justin's background in comedy, internet culture, and editing has led him to reach millions of viewers online with his content, while Chris's unwavering commitment to equality and social justice drives his work.

But it's not just our founders who make Left Ahead unique. As an employee-owned business, we believe in empowering our team members to bring their own creativity and unique perspectives to the table. We're not just about creating political content - we're about bringing internet culture to the world of political communications.

What We Do

Our content creation services are designed to captivate your audience and amplify your message. We specialize in producing compelling videos that resonate with your target audience, combining elements of satire, comedy, and culture jamming.

From scriptwriting and storyboarding to video production and editing, our team brings your vision to life with creativity and precision. With our content, you can inspire conversations, drive engagement, and make a lasting impact.

Collaboration is at the heart of what we do. We believe in the power of partnerships to amplify our collective impact. Through strategic collaborations, we join forces with like-minded organizations, political figures, and influencers who share our vision of driving positive change.

By combining our creative expertise and resources, we can reach a wider audience, spark meaningful conversations, and inspire action. Let's work together to create a powerful partnership that makes a real difference.

Our consultation services provide valuable insights and guidance to help you navigate the complex world of political communications. Whether you're seeking advice on campaign strategies, audience engagement tactics, or creative direction, our experienced consultants are here to assist you.

We offer strategic planning, audience analysis, and performance tracking to ensure your campaigns are effective and impactful. With our consultation services, you can gain a fresh perspective, refine your messaging, and achieve your goals with confidence.
